Healthy Street Food Choices

Street food is delicious and part of culture. Here are practical tips to choose healthier options at stalls:

  • Prefer steamed or grilled items (momos, kebabs) over deep-fried ones.
  • Choose smaller portions — try 1 piece instead of 3.
  • Add a side of salad or buttermilk for balance.
  • Look for vendors who use less oil and fresher ingredients.
